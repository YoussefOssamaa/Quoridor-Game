# Quoridor-Game
Quoridor is an abstract strategy board game played on a 9Ã—9 board. The objective is to be the first player to move their pawn to the opposite side. Players move their pawn or place one of their 10 walls. Walls are two squares long and must not completely block a player's path to their goal.
The game has a human x human mode, and an AI vs Human mode with 3 different difficulties.
